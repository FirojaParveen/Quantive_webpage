WHY A BUSINESS VALUATION

For most entrepreneurs, their company is their largest asset.  For over 15 years entrepreneurs have entrusted our professionals advice and consultation on a wide range of business valuation matters.   Our professionals are accredited in business valuation and provide valuation support for transaction support, tax matters, litigation matters, and business planning needs.

Quantive completes hundreds of valuation assignments every year.  We have a nationwide footprint – on average we work in about 40 states.  We’re fortunate that with our scale we have experience in and exposure to a truly wide range of industries and business models.

We are routinely engaged for valuation assignments relating to:

  • M&A Transaction Support (selling or acquiring a company)
  • 409(a) Valuations
  • Exit Planning / Pre-exit Analysis
  • SBA Loans
  • Buy-sell Agreements
  • Trust and Estate Valuation Services
  • Litigation Support (commercial litigation, shareholder disputes)
  • Divorce Matters
  • Financial Reporting

BUSINESS VALUATION PROCESS

We live by a simple mantra: a repeatable process yields repeatable results.  While every assignment is different, we follow the same general outline to help ensure consistent, high quality work.  

Get a Quote

We'll do a quick discovery call to understand the assignment and offer a written quote.   For most of our work we provide fixed fee pricing.  

Engage Quantive

We'll do a quick discovery call to understand the assignment and offer a written quote.   For most of our work we provide fixed fee pricing.  

Data Request

To perform the valuation we'll need access to a range of financial and operating information. We'll provide a thorough list, and work with you regarding alternatives on items that you might not have.  

Secure Document Delivery

We're serious about confidentiality and security.  We'd strongly prefer not to receive data via email - rather through a secure portal that we'll share with you.  

First Pass" Analysis

Once we have data in we'll do what we call a "first pass" analysis- this includes looking at ratios, trends, and "red flags" that we'll want to get more granular on. 

Management Inteview

We are firm believers that the numbers only tell part of the story.  We want to understand the narrative behind the numbers:  how did you get here, and why?  This helps us drill down on how your company fits in the range of value across industries and amongst peers.

"Deep Dive" Analysis

Here we marry the narrative and the numbers.  Now that we have an understanding of the dynamics that are driving performance we can get more granular.  Oftentimes we wind up iterating this process - we learn things in the management interview, ask for more data, and repeat until we've got clarity on the matter.  

Final Drafting and Brief Out

We complete our analysis by documenting all of our findings into a detailed report.  Unless otherwise directed or agreed to, our report will comply with NACVA reporting standards.  We ensure our work product meets various reporting requirements- which might include SBA SOP guidance, be considered a "Qualified Appraisal" by the IRS, or other requirements as needed.  

We'll deliver our report, and also conduct a brief out with you to discuss findings, observations, and what actions might be next.
